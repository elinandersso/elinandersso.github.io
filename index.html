Milan Design Week

UX design + web editing

Ingka Group Annual Summary
& Sustainability Report

I designed and edited the web version of the Ingka Group Annual Summary and Sustainability Report FY25 on ingka.com with a new design and content structure adapted for ESG reporting and a new on-site navigation with improved user experience, were a new page for all Ingka Group reports was created.

The project required close collaboration with the developers to implement the reports page, with reporting and sustainability stakeholders to create the web version and publish the digital report, and coordination with the social media team ahead of go live.

Web content + SEO

IKEA Second-hand Marketplace

To support the launch of the IKEA Second-hand Marketplace, a new digital experience was created for the IKEA e-retail website. The new second-hand platform was not indexed for search, making it crucial to deliver search optimised content to IKEA.com as a gateway and traffic-driver for increased visibility for customers, without competing with the core retail journey.

Led the full global web content delivery for IKEA.com, including web and UX design in Figma, SEO optimisation and performance, and stakeholder alignment across Online Merchandising, Sustainability, Strategy, Development and Innovation, and Digital teams.

Results: Delivered a global content and URL structure recommendation with a full content package and web guidelines to five markets in the first roll-out in 2025 (continuing in 2026).
